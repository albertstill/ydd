The YDD Manifesto

The concept at the core of YDD is very simpleâ€”in fact, it's just four letters: #YOLO.

We, the proponents of YDD, have made it our way of life. When you're writing software, what's the point in taking the long path? Ain't nobody got time for that! Life is short, and you need to ship before you get old.

The principles of YDD

Here are the five principles of #YOLO Driven Development:

  1. Write as little code as possible, even if it means leaving out features.
  2. Don't worry about testing your code. Good code tests itself.
  3. Deploy a new version of your app every hour.
  4. Don't use complex software that will slow you down.
  5. Don't worry about learning new technologies. There's no time for that.

Success Stories

The lives of those who have embraced YDD have improved drastically. Many have come out of depression, lots of them have found a job, and an even bigger number of people have found just what life's worth living for.
